Display real-time flight information based on the OpenSky REST API.
Hover a plane to get its callsign. The second plot is automatically updated to display its altitude group.
You can also go the other way around and click directly on the altitude category to filter the map display.
Eventually, you can stop the automatic update every 10s by clicking on the On/Off update button.
